Instructions

**Please read carefully**

1. Students must fill all the details correctly and in the given format.

2. Please enter your entrance exam roll number/application number and rank(if applicable) correctly.

3. Students should not include any salutations before your name and please write your complete name.

4. Enter your name without any special characters. (*for example:if name is V.N. Jha then write V N Jha) .

5. Please do not include any STD code before your contact number and enter your 10 digit mobile number not landline number.

6. Please write your complete and latest address.

7. Size of the scanned photo must be less than 40kb and scanned signatures must be less than 10kb.

8. All the images must be clear and upright.

9. Extension of the images should be .jpeg,.png,.gif.
